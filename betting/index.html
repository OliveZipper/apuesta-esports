What is Esports Betting?

Esports betting allows you to wager on the outcomes of professional video game competitions. Just like traditional sports betting, you can bet on match winners, tournament champions, and various in-game events. The esports betting market has grown exponentially, with millions wagered on major tournaments like The International, CS2 Majors, and the League of Legends World Championship.

What makes esports betting unique is the depth of game-specific knowledge that can give you an edge. Understanding meta shifts, team dynamics, map pools, and player form can significantly improve your predictions. Our guides are designed to help you understand these nuances for each major esport.

Common Bet Types Explained

Understanding the different ways you can bet on esports matches

Match Winner (Moneyline)

The simplest bet type. Pick which team will win the match. No spreads, no handicaps‚Äîjust straight-up who wins.

Example
NAVI to beat G2 @ 1.75

Map Winner

Bet on the winner of a specific map within a match. Useful when you like an underdog's chances on their best map.

Example
Spirit to win Map 2 @ 2.10

Handicap (Spread)

Give or take away maps/rounds from a team to even the odds. Great for matches with heavy favorites.

Example
Underdog +1.5 maps @ 1.65

Totals (Over/Under)

Bet on whether the total maps, rounds, or kills will be over or under a specified number.

Example
Over 2.5 maps @ 1.85

Prop Bets

Wager on specific in-game events: first blood, first tower, total kills, ace rounds, and more.

Example

Outright (Futures)

Bet on tournament winners or season-long outcomes. Higher risk but potentially huge returns.

Example
TI Winner - Team Spirit @ 8.00

Esports betting should be entertainment, not a source of income. The house always has an edge, and no amount of knowledge guarantees wins. Before you bet, make sure you understand the risks.

  • Only bet what you can afford to lose
  • Set a budget and stick to it
  • Never chase losses
  • Take breaks regularly
  • Don't bet under the influence
  • Keep betting fun, not stressful
