Common Betting Markets

Match Winner

The most straightforward bet - pick which team wins the match.

Map Handicap

Bet on a team to win with a map advantage or disadvantage applied.

Total Maps

Predict whether a match goes over or under a certain number of maps.

First Blood

Bet on which team scores the first kill in a game or map.

Round Handicap

In CS2, bet on round differential with handicaps applied.

Correct Score

Predict the exact map score of a match (2-0, 2-1, etc.).

Tournament Winner

Outright bet on which team wins the entire tournament.

Player Props

Bet on individual player performance metrics.
