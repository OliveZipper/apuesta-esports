# SEO & Promotion Plan - Apuesta Esports

## Executive Summary
Comprehensive strategy to drive organic traffic, build domain authority, and establish apuestaesports.com as a trusted esports betting resource.

---

## 1. Backlink Strategy

### High-Value Targets

#### Esports News Sites (Guest Posts)
| Site | DA | Approach |
|------|-----|----------|
| Esports.net | 55 | Pitch original data/analysis pieces |
| Dot Esports | 70 | Offer exclusive tournament previews |
| HLTV.org | 75 | CS2 betting market analysis |
| Liquipedia | 72 | Contribute missing data, earn citations |
| TheScore Esports | 60 | Player profile collaborations |

#### Gaming Forums & Communities
- **Reddit**: r/esports (1M+), r/DotA2 (1M+), r/GlobalOffensive (2M+), r/leagueoflegends (6M+)
  - Strategy: Share genuinely helpful content, not promotional. Build karma first.
  - Target threads: "Where can I learn about esports betting?" "Best resources for X game"
- **Discord Communities**: Official game discords, team fan servers
  - Join, contribute value, share resources when relevant
- **Steam Community**: Game hubs, guides section

#### Resource Link Building
- Create linkable assets:
  - **Infographics**: "History of TI Prize Pools", "Esports Earnings by Country"
  - **Tools**: Odds calculator, bankroll tracker spreadsheet
  - **Data**: Player earnings database, team rankings tracker
- Pitch to educational sites, Wikipedia citations

## 2. Paid Promotion Options

### Google Ads
**Budget**: $500-2000/month testing phase

#### Campaign Structure
1. **Search Campaigns**
   - Keywords: "esports betting guide", "how to bet on [game]", "[tournament] betting odds"
   - Avoid: Generic gambling terms (expensive, low intent)
   - Geo-target: Legal markets (UK, Malta, Philippines, LATAM)

2. **Display/YouTube**
   - Target: Esports content viewers, gaming channels
   - Retargeting: Site visitors who didn't convert

#### Landing Pages
- Create dedicated pages for each ad group
- A/B test: Educational content vs. odds comparison

### Social Media Ads

#### Twitter/X
- Target: Esports followers, team/player accounts
- Content: Tournament predictions, betting tips threads
- Budget: $200-500/month

#### TikTok
- Short-form betting tips, "Did you know" esports facts
- Target: 18-34 gaming audience
- Budget: $300-800/month

### Affiliate Networks
- Join esports affiliate networks to cross-promote
- Consider: Better Collective, Catena Media partnerships

## 3. PR Opportunities

### Press Release Strategy

#### Newsworthy Angles
1. **Data Releases**: "Esports Prize Pools Hit $X Billion in 2026"
2. **Predictions**: Pre-tournament analysis with odds breakdown
3. **Industry Commentary**: Regulatory changes, new markets opening
4. **Original Research**: Survey esports bettors, publish findings

#### Distribution
- PR Newswire / Business Wire (for major releases)
- Free: PRLog, OpenPR, PR.com
- Direct pitches to gaming journalists
