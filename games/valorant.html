üèÜ VALORANT Champions Tour - The Global Circuit

The VCT is Valorant's official esports ecosystem, featuring partnered teams across three international leagues: Americas, EMEA, and Pacific. Teams compete in regional splits throughout the year, qualifying for international events like Masters and Champions.

VCT Champions is the world championship, held annually with teams from all regions competing for the title. LOUD (Brazil) won Champions 2022, while Evil Geniuses (NA) took the 2023 crown, showcasing the global nature of competitive Valorant.

About Valorant

Tactical precision meets unique abilities

üìñ History & Growth

Valorant launched in June 2020, developed by Riot Games as their entry into the tactical shooter genre. Combining the precise gunplay of Counter-Strike with unique agent abilities similar to Overwatch, it quickly attracted millions of players and CS professionals.

The game's esports scene started with community tournaments before Riot launched the official VCT in 2021. The franchised league system began in 2023, with 30 partnered teams across Americas, EMEA, and Pacific regions receiving guaranteed spots and revenue sharing.

Notable storylines include Sentinels' early dominance with TenZ, LOUD's rise as Brazil's first FPS world champions, and Fnatic's consistent international success.
