üìú Team History

FaZe Clan began in 2010 as a Call of Duty sniping and trickshot YouTube channel. The organization evolved from content creation into a legitimate esports powerhouse, becoming one of the most valuable and recognizable brands in gaming culture.

The organization expanded into competitive esports across multiple titles including CS:GO, Call of Duty, Fortnite, and Valorant. Their CS:GO team achieved the ultimate goal at PGL Antwerp Major 2022, winning their first-ever Major championship with an international roster featuring karrigan, ropz, Twistzz, rain, and broky.

FaZe Clan went public on NASDAQ in 2022, becoming one of the first esports organizations to be publicly traded. Beyond esports, FaZe has become a lifestyle brand with clothing lines, celebrity collaborations, and one of the largest gaming influencer networks in the world.
