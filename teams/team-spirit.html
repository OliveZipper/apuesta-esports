üìú History

Team Spirit is a Russian esports organization founded in 2015. The team shocked the world by winning The International 2021 (TI10) as massive underdogs, defeating the heavily favored PSG.LGD in the grand finals.

The organization originally competed in various titles including League of Legends and CS:GO before focusing primarily on Dota 2. Their TI10-winning roster, featuring young talents like Yatoro, TORONTOTOKYO, and Collapse, became overnight sensations.

What made their TI10 victory special was the underdog narrative ‚Äì the team had formed just months before the tournament and was given little chance against the dominant Chinese teams. Their aggressive, fearless playstyle captivated audiences worldwide.

Team Spirit continued their dominance by winning TI13 in 2024, becoming only the second organization (after OG) to win multiple Internationals, cementing their legacy in Dota 2 history.
