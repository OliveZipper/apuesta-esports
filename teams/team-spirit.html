üìú Team History

Team Spirit was founded in 2015 as a Russian esports organization competing primarily in Dota 2 and Counter-Strike. For years, the team operated as a solid but not exceptional presence in the CIS region, developing young talent and competing in regional leagues.

Everything changed at The International 2021 (TI10), where Team Spirit emerged as the ultimate underdogs. Entering as one of the lowest-ranked teams, they defeated PSG.LGD in a thrilling grand finals to claim the Aegis of Champions and $18.2 million in prize money. Their run was marked by incredible teamwork and clutch plays from carry player YATORO.

Team Spirit continued their dominance by winning The International 2023 (TI12), joining OG as the only organizations to win multiple Internationals. Their CS2 division has also become a top-tier contender in the tactical shooter scene.
