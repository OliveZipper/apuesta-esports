üìú Team History

OG was founded in October 2015 by Johan "N0tail" Sundstein, one of the most decorated players in Dota 2 history. The organization quickly established itself as a dominant force in competitive Dota 2, winning back-to-back Majors in their first season.

The team's greatest achievement came in 2018 and 2019, when they became the only organization to win The International twice in a row. Their TI8 run was considered a Cinderella story, entering as underdogs after significant roster changes, while their TI9 victory cemented their legacy as the greatest Dota 2 team of all time.

OG has since expanded into CS:GO and Valorant, though their Dota 2 legacy remains their defining achievement. The organization is known for their innovative playstyle, mental resilience, and the iconic "flower" celebration.
