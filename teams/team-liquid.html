üìú Team History

Team Liquid was founded in 2000 as a StarCraft: Brood War community website in the Netherlands. The organization transitioned into a professional esports team, becoming one of the most successful and recognizable brands in competitive gaming history.

In 2015, Team Liquid merged with Team Curse, bringing their League of Legends franchise into the fold. This merger, backed by aXiomatic esports (co-owned by Michael Jordan and Steve Aoki), transformed Liquid into a multi-game powerhouse.

Throughout their history, Liquid has achieved remarkable success across multiple titles including Dota 2 (TI7 Champions), CS:GO (Intel Grand Slam), StarCraft II, League of Legends, and many more. Their Dota 2 roster's victory at The International 2017 remains one of the organization's crowning achievements.
