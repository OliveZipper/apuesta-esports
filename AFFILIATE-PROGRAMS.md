### Disclosure Requirements
- Add affiliate disclosure to footer
- Mark affiliate links with rel="sponsored"
- Comply with FTC guidelines
- Regional gambling ad restrictions

### Tracking Setup
1. Create separate tracking IDs per page/section
2. Use UTM parameters for internal tracking
3. Set up conversion tracking in analytics
4. Monthly reconciliation of commissions
