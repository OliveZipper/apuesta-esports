Yaroslav "Miposhka" Naidenov is a Russian Dota 2 captain who led Team Spirit to one of the most stunning underdog victories in esports history at The International 2021. His strategic brilliance and calm leadership helped transform a team of relatively unknown players into world champions.

Miposhka's approach to the game emphasizes team coordination, innovative drafting, and mental resilience. Under his captaincy, Team Spirit became known for their aggressive early-game strategies and ability to maintain composure in high-pressure situations.

His victory at TI10 against PSG.LGD in a thrilling 3-2 series cemented his legacy as one of the greatest captains in Dota 2 history, proving that teamwork and strategy can overcome individually superior opponents.
