üìñ About Topson

Topias "Topson" Taavitsainen is a Finnish Dota 2 midlaner whose unorthodox playstyle revolutionized how the Position 2 role is played. He became a two-time TI champion in his first two TI appearances, a feat never accomplished before or since.

Topson was a relatively unknown pub player before joining OG in 2018, known for his unusual hero picks and aggressive, tempo-controlling playstyle. His willingness to pick unconventional mid heroes and dominate lanes through sheer mechanical skill caught opponents off-guard throughout OG's historic runs.

His confidence and creativity in high-pressure situations became a defining characteristic of OG's playstyle. Topson's ability to find kills and create chaos across the map allowed ana to farm freely while keeping opponents constantly on the defensive.

üéØ Playstyle & Role

Topson's playstyle is characterized by aggression, creativity, and an almost supernatural ability to win mid lanes with unusual picks. He pioneered mid-lane builds and heroes that other players considered off-meta, forcing opponents to respect his innovation.

His tempo-controlling approach focuses on creating chaos across the map rather than static farming. Topson excels at punishing opponents' mistakes and snowballing small advantages into complete map control, often drawing multiple heroes to deal with him while his team capitalizes elsewhere.
