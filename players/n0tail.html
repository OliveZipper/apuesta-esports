Johan "N0tail" Sundstein is a Danish professional Dota 2 player widely regarded as one of the greatest support players and captains in esports history. He is the co-founder and long-time captain of OG, one of the most successful Dota 2 organizations ever.

N0tail holds the distinction of being the highest-earning esports player of all time, primarily thanks to OG's back-to-back victories at The International 2018 and 2019 ‚Äî an unprecedented achievement that cemented his legacy in competitive gaming.

Before his Dota 2 career, N0tail was a top Heroes of Newerth player. His transition to Dota 2 began with Fnatic in 2012, and he has been competing at the highest level ever since.
