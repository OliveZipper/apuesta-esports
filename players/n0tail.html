üìñ About N0tail

Johan "N0tail" Sundstein is a Danish professional Dota 2 player and the highest-earning esports player in history. Known for his exceptional leadership and innovative drafting, N0tail captained OG to back-to-back victories at The International 2018 and 2019, an unprecedented achievement in esports.

Before transitioning to Dota 2, N0tail was a professional Heroes of Newerth player, where he was considered one of the best players in the world. His versatility allowed him to play multiple positions throughout his career, eventually settling into the support role where he became the strategic mastermind behind OG's success.

N0tail is renowned for his positive attitude, flower-power philosophy, and ability to foster team synergy. He co-founded OG in 2015 and built it into the most successful Dota 2 organization in history. His leadership style emphasizes mental fortitude and team chemistry over pure mechanical skill.
