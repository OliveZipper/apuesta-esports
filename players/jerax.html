üìñ Biography

Jesse "JerAx" Vainikka is a Finnish professional Dota 2 player widely considered one of the greatest position 4 supports in the game's history. His mechanical skill on playmaking heroes and clutch performances in high-pressure moments made him a cornerstone of OG's legendary roster.

JerAx joined OG in 2016 and became instrumental in their dominance of the Major circuit and their unprecedented back-to-back TI victories in 2018 and 2019. His partnership with N0tail in the support duo became one of the most successful in Dota 2 history.

After retiring from competitive Dota 2 in early 2020, JerAx briefly returned to competition but has since stepped back from professional play, leaving behind a legacy as one of the most mechanically gifted support players ever.
