üìñ About JerAx

Jesse "JerAx" Vainikka is widely regarded as the greatest Position 4 player in Dota 2 history. His exceptional playmaking ability, mechanical precision, and clutch performances were instrumental in OG's back-to-back victories at The International 2018 and 2019.

JerAx's Earth Spirit is legendary in the Dota 2 community, with many considering it the best in the world. His ability to execute complex combos under pressure and make game-winning plays in crucial moments earned him the nickname "the Finnish Flash."

After achieving unparalleled success with OG, JerAx announced his retirement from competitive Dota 2 in 2020 to focus on personal projects and his well-being. His departure left a void in the Position 4 role that many argue has never been truly filled.
