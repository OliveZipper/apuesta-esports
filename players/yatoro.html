üìñ About Yatoro

Ilya "Yatoro" Mulyarchuk is a Ukrainian Dota 2 prodigy who became a TI champion at just 18 years old. His explosive debut at The International 2021 showcased a player with exceptional mechanical skill and an impossibly deep hero pool.

Yatoro holds the record for playing the most unique heroes at a single TI, demonstrating versatility that made Team Spirit's drafts nearly impossible to predict. His ability to master any carry hero made him the centerpiece of Spirit's championship run.

Despite his young age, Yatoro plays with the composure of a veteran, making clutch plays under pressure and maintaining consistent performance throughout grueling tournament runs. His emergence marks the beginning of a new generation of Dota 2 talent.
