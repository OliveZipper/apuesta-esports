Kuro "KuroKy" Takhasomi is a German Dota 2 legend and one of the most accomplished players in esports history. With a career spanning from the original DotA to Dota 2, KuroKy has been a top-tier competitor for over 15 years.

KuroKy captained Team Liquid to victory at The International 2017, completing an incredible lower bracket run that saw the team defeat Newbee 3-0 in one of the most dominant Grand Finals in TI history. His strategic mind and leadership transformed Liquid into a dynasty.

Throughout his career, KuroKy has played every position at the highest level, from carry to support. His versatility, game knowledge, and ability to adapt to any meta have made him a constant presence in the upper echelons of competitive Dota 2.
